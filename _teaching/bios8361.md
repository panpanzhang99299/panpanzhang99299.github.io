**Fall 2025**

This course (formerly BIOS 7361) is the first in a two-course series on advanced probability and statistical inference. Topics include but not limited to
* Characteristic functions
* Modes of convergence
* Limit theorems
* Martingales
* Markov chains
* Branching processes
* Random networks
